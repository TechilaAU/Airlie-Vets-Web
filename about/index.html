About Our Practice

Dedicated to your pet's health and happiness

Airlie Beach Veterinary Surgery has been serving the Cannonvale and wider Whitsunday community for over 30 years. In that time, we've cared for thousands of pets — from cats and dogs to guinea pigs, birds and even a few snakes.

Our clinic is built on the belief that excellent veterinary care starts with a genuine love of animals. Every member of our team shares that passion — with patience, warmth and thoroughness.

We know that bringing your pet to the vet can be stressful. That's why we work hard to create a calm, welcoming environment where pets feel at ease and owners feel informed and supported.
