    About Our Practice

    Dedicated to your pet's health and happiness

    Airlie Beach Veterinary Surgery has been serving the Cannonvale and wider Whitsunday community for over 30 years. In that time, we've cared for thousands of pets — from cats and dogs to guinea pigs, birds and even a few snakes.

    Our clinic is built on the belief that excellent veterinary care starts with a genuine love of animals. Every member of our team shares that passion, and it shows in the way we approach every consultation — with patience, warmth and thoroughness.

    We know that bringing your pet to the vet can be stressful. That's why we work hard to create a calm, welcoming environment where pets feel at ease and owners feel informed and supported.

    Our Approach

    What sets us apart

    We bring together modern medicine, genuine compassion and a deep commitment to our community.

    Advice First

    We believe informed pet owners make the best decisions. We take time to explain health issues clearly and outline all available treatment options without pressure.

    Modern Facilities

    Our clinic is equipped with modern diagnostic machines, digital X-ray, ultrasound and in-house pathology for fast, accurate results.

    Genuine Care

    Every person on our team is a true animal lover. We treat your pets as if they were our own — with patience, gentleness and real affection.

    Community Roots

    We've grown alongside the Cannonvale and Whitsunday community for three decades. Our clients trust us because we've earned it, visit by visit.

    Affordable Care

    Quality veterinary care shouldn't be out of reach. We work hard to keep our services professional and thorough while remaining accessible to all.

    7-Day After-Hours

    We now offer 7-day and after-hours veterinary services, because your pet's health doesn't follow a 9-to-5 schedule.
