The Whitsundays Vet Clinic

Caring for The Whitsundays
pets for 30+ years

Airlie Beach Veterinary Surgery has served pets and their families across the Whitsundays region for over 30 years. From Airlie Beach and Cannonvale to Jubilee Pocket, Proserpine and beyond, we're the region's most experienced and trusted vet clinic.

The Whitsundays is a unique place to live — and our pets are part of what makes it special. We're proud to have grown alongside this community, caring for thousands of animals and building lifelong relationships with pet owners across the region.

We offer a comprehensive range of services including health checks, vaccinations, desexing, orthopaedic and soft tissue surgery, dentistry, microchipping, radiography, ultrasonography and grooming — all under one roof.
