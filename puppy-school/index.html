Puppy School

A friendly, small-group start to life for your new puppy — run by our own veterinary nurses right here in Cannonvale.

Puppy Preschool

The best start for your new best mate

The weeks between 8 and 16 weeks of age are the most important in a puppy's life. What they meet, hear and experience now shapes the calm, confident, well-mannered dog they grow into. Our puppy school makes the most of that window in a safe, controlled environment supervised by our nursing team.

Classes are kept deliberately small so every puppy — and every owner — gets attention. You will leave each session with practical skills to use at home and the reassurance of having your questions answered by people who see puppies every day.

Enrichment

Why puppy school matters

A puppy's brain is primed for learning about the world during their first few months. Puppies who are well socialised during this time are far less likely to develop fear, anxiety and aggression as adults — the behavioural problems that sadly lead many dogs to be surrendered. A few weeks of the right experiences now pays off for the whole life of your dog, and gives you a calmer, more confident companion.
