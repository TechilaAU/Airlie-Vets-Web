Jubilee Pocket Vet Clinic

Caring for Jubilee Pocket
pets for 30+ years

If you live in Jubilee Pocket, Airlie Beach Veterinary Surgery is your closest and most experienced local vet. Located just minutes away at 58 Shute Harbour Road, Cannonvale, we've cared for Jubilee Pocket pets for over 30 years.

We understand the strong bond between Jubilee Pocket families and their pets. That's why we offer flexible appointment times and 24/7 on-call availability — so help is always available when your pet needs it most.

We offer comprehensive services including health checks, vaccinations, desexing, orthopaedic and soft tissue surgery, dentistry, microchipping, radiography, ultrasonography and grooming — all under one roof.
